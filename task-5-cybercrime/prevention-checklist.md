# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (India)
## 1. Secure Your Accounts
Use strong, unique passwords for each app
Turn on Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) (especially email, banking, socials).
## 2. Lock Down Your Devices
Set screen lock (PIN/biometric)
Install apps only from official stores
Keep OS and apps updated.
## 3. Avoid Suspicious Links
Don’t click unknown links from SMS/DMs/emails
Check URLs carefully (fake domains look similar).
## 4. Think Before You Share
Avoid posting ID cards, tickets, location in real-time
Limit personal info (phone, address, college details).
## 5. Verify Before Trusting
Don’t trust urgent requests (“send money now”)
Confirm via a call on the official number before acting.
## 6. UPI Safety – Never Share Sensitive Info
Never share UPI PIN, OTP, CVV, or passwords with anyone
No bank/UPI app (like Google Pay or PhonePe) will ask for your PIN.
## 7. UPI Safety – Check Requests Carefully
“Collect/Request Money” means you are paying, not receiving
Verify receiver name/UPI ID before approving
Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources.
## 8. Use Safe Networks
Avoid logging into banking/UPI on public Wi-Fi
If needed, use mobile data or a trusted network.
## 9. Control App Permissions
Review permissions (camera, contacts, storage)
Remove access for apps that don’t need it.
## 10. Spot & Block Scams
Beware of fake internships, giveaway traps, job scams
Block/report suspicious accounts immediately.
## 11. Report Cyber Fraud Immediately
File a complaint on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
Call Helpline 1930 for quick action (especially for financial fraud).
