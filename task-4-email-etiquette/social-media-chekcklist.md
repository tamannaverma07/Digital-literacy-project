# Social Media Do's 
 ## 1. Keep Your Account Safe
Make sure your passwords are secure and distinct.
Turn on two-factor verification (2FA).
Update your privacy settings. 
 ## 2. Consider Your Posting
Post only what is necessary, courteous, and true.
Keep in mind that your digital footprint is permanent.
Be aware of the impact your content has on other people. 
 ## 3. Confirm Data
Verify information before sharing
Make use of verified accounts and reliable sources.
Verify news from several sources. 
 ## 4. Show Respect
Talk courteously
Respect differing viewpoints.
 ## 5. Report cyberbullying rather than participate in it 📸
Give the original creators credit.
Before reposting, get permission.
Distribute insightful and uplifting content.
## 6. Keep Yourself Safe Online
Do not click on dubious links.
Report or block harmful or fraudulent accounts
Watch out for fraudulent giveaways and scams.

# Don'ts on Social Media
## 1. Don't divulge too much personal information
Don't share your address, phone number, or passwords.
Don't divulge personal financial or family information.
## 2. Avoid Disseminating False Information
Don't spread unreliable information.
Refrain from disseminating false information or rumors.
## 3. Avoid Toxic Behavior
No hate speech, trolling, or cyberbullying
Avoid getting into heated online arguments.
## 4. Don't Believe Everything You See Online
Not every profile is authentic.
Watch out for phony identities and strangers.
## 5. Avoid Clicking on Unknown Links
Steer clear of dubious downloads, links, and advertisements.
Avoid opening messages from unidentified sources.
## 6. Avoid Posting While Angry
Don't post when you're feeling upset.
Consider your response before responding to remarks.
